About the Work

A Masterwork of Catholic Mystical Theology

Father J. Ribet's La Mystique Divine stands as one of the most thorough systematic treatments of Catholic mystical theology ever written. Drawing on Scripture, the Fathers, and the great mystics of the Church, Ribet maps the interior life with rare precision and depth.

This edition presents the complete four-volume work in English, making Ribet's scholarship accessible to the contemporary reader seeking to understand the heights of contemplative life.
