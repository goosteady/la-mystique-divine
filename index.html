The Four Volumes

  1. Volume I Contemplation The nature, degrees, causes, and effects of mystical prayer — the soul's ascent toward union with God.
  2. Volume II The Mystical Phenomena Distinct from Contemplation Visions, locutions, revelations, and the extraordinary graces that accompany the mystical life.
  3. Volume III The Diabolical Counterfeits Discernment of spirits — the marks by which divine mysticism is distinguished from its counterfeits.
  4. Volume IV The Human Analogies The natural counterparts of mystical experience — the phenomena of body and soul that resemble, without attaining, the divine.
About this edition. Canon J. Ribet's La Mystique Divine is one of the most thorough systematic treatments of Catholic mystical theology ever written. This edition presents a complete machine-assisted draft translation of the 1895 Poussielgue edition in four volumes — a working draft, not a critical edition.
